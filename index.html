about us

burning with the idea

Chitrika is the official photography forum of Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology. It aims to promote the creativity and gives an exposure to the talent of photography among the students. Termed as the ‘Lens of VBIT’, Chitrika fosters the interest of the students who are passionate about Photography. It provides a broader outlook to photography and inculcates the awareness about photography among the students by providing them a closer and a deeper view.
