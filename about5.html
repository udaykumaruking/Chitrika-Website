Vision

The vision of Chitrika is to encourage and engage the interest of the students in enhancing their passion of photography by providing them various platforms to showcase their talent. It aims to develop and enhance the photography skills among the young and talented photographers. The focus is to promote the talent of photography of all the students and throw more light on the subject of pursuing photography as a passion beyond the career as well.

About Photography

The history of photography is an exciting history of the origin and the realization of the dreams of fixing and long-term preservation of images, which surround us, and the phenomena of objects. It is one of the brightest and most stormy phases of the development of modern information technology. Looking upon the past of photography, we can estimate the enormous impact, which it has had on the development of modern culture, science and technology. The history of photography, unlike studies in other types of arts with a long tradition, began to develop only in last time, precisely in the recent years.
